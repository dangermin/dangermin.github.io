Secureboxlive™ is a new development in firearm security that provides firearm owners a new level of peace of mind. The device provides its user with security alerts when their firearm is moved, accessed, tampered with or stolen – in real time, within seconds.

The alerts provided by Secureboxlive™ to its user’s email, computer, phone or other mobile device acts as a deterrent to children who might access firearms. Just knowing the alerts will occur prevents curious children from attempting to unlock a case and access a firearm. The alerts also provide quick notification if a firearm is tampered with or stolen. Over 100,000 firearms are carried by children to school each year. A Secureboxlive™ owner can answer the question “Its 10:00 AM, where is my firearm?” Bottom line - Secureboxlive™ can save lives.
