About Us

MVP Group is a product research, development, and marketing company with a focus toward integrating wireless technology into commonly used devices, products and applications. We have developed unique patent pending wireless notification intelligence applications which can and have been applied in a variety of products.

SECUREBOXLIVE™ was envisioned with cutting edge storage practices in addition to quick access of your firearm(s) when needed. We provide locking holster cases without the worry of lockout due to technical failure. Premium holster cases include Cellular and Wi-Fi alert capabilities to provide location of the holster case (or your vehicle if the case/unit is in the vehicle) at a minimal cost and give you unprecedented protection in 144 different countries around the world. The bottom line, we help you “Protect the Ones You Love” and “Protect the Things You Love” and provide peace of mind, protection and security at home or on the go.

Our focus is on increasing safety and security by eliminating variables. We believe that our superior technology makes interaction with holsters and cases effective by providing an affordable and simple platform for real time reporting and feedback when you want it.
